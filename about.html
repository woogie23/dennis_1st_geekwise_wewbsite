My About Page!

Here's a little bit about me

I've been retired for a few years now, after starting out as a very young person and working my way through all kinds of stuff that happen in life. After a long and storied professional career, it has been very satisfying to become very involved in Photography, and to return back to school, just for the sake of learning new things that interest me. "The Hunt for Red October" has been my favorite movie for many years, I need to sit down and enjoy it again soon. My favorite food or dinner would likely be Porkchops with mashed potatoes and gravy, just because it is comforting an tastes so good. Don't tell anyone, but I think I may be a closet nerd, as learning nerdy things is more fun for me, more than I should let anyone else know.
